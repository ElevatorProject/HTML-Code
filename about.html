Project VI - About

Welcome to the About page for Project VI .
The purpose of this project is to build and control an elevator, using a CAN bus communications environment,
coupled with networked systems to create a means of tracking the elevator's operational/diagnostics data.
Additionally, the elevator car's position and status is viewable to computers over the internet.
We are really excited that you are here to witness history in the making!
